Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zermatt

Arrive in Zermatt and check in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day exploring the charming village and getting acclimated.

Day 2: Matterhorn Excursion

Take a cable car ride up to the Matterhorn Glacier Paradise for breathtaking views of the iconic mountain and surrounding peaks. Optional: Skiing or snowboarding.

Day 3: Travel to Interlaken

Travel to Interlaken by train. Check into your hotel and explore the area, known for its stunning lakes and adventurous activities.

Day 4: Adventure Sports in Interlaken

Enjoy a day of adventure sports including paragliding, canyoning, or bungee jumping. Return to your hotel for a relaxing evening.

Day 5: Travel to Lucerne

Travel to Lucerne by train. Check into your hotel and explore the medieval architecture and lakeside scenery.

Day 6: Exploring Lucerne

Visit the Chapel Bridge, the Lion Monument, and take a boat trip on Lake Lucerne. Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 7: Departure

Check out from your hotel and depart for the airport. End of the tour.
