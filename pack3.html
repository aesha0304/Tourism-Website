Lakeside Escape

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
Destinations: Geneva, Montreux, Lausanne
Activities:
Explore the beautiful city of Geneva and visit the United Nations Headquarters.
Discover the vineyards and castles around Lake Geneva in Montreux.
Enjoy a boat trip on Lake Geneva and visit the Chillon Castle.
Experience the cultural heritage of Lausanne and stroll along the lakeside promenade.
Accommodation: 4-star hotels with breakfast included.
Price: Starting from $1200 per person.
