About Interlaken

Interlaken is a vibrant town located in the heart of the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland. Situated between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, it is renowned for its breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, including the iconic Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks. The town serves as a popular base for outdoor activities such as skiing, hiking, and paragliding.

Visitors to Interlaken can take a boat cruise on the pristine lakes, ride the funicular to Harder Kulm for panoramic views, or embark on an unforgettable journey to the Jungfraujoch, the Top of Europe. The region offers a perfect blend of adventure sports and serene landscapes, making it a favorite destination for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.
