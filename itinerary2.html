Swiss Bliss Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich

Arrive in Zurich and check in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day exploring the city's vibrant culture and historical landmarks.

Day 2: Explore Zurich

Discover Zurich's Old Town, visit the Swiss National Museum, and enjoy a relaxing evening by Lake Zurich.

Day 3: Travel to Bern

Take a train to Bern, Switzerland's capital. Check into your hotel and start exploring the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Old Town.

Day 4: Explore Bern

Visit the Zytglogge clock tower, the Federal Palace, and enjoy a stroll along the Aare River.

Day 5: Travel to Zermatt

Travel to Zermatt by train. Check into your hotel and spend the rest of the day exploring the charming village.

Day 6: Matterhorn Excursion

Take the Gornergrat Railway for stunning views of the Matterhorn and surrounding peaks. Optional: Skiing or snowboarding.

Day 7: Travel to Jungfraujoch

Travel to Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe". Experience the Aletsch Glacier and breathtaking alpine views.

Day 8: Explore Jungfraujoch

Spend the day exploring the various attractions and activities available at Jungfraujoch.

Day 9: Travel to Geneva

Take a train to Geneva. Check into your hotel and start exploring the city's beautiful lakeside and international landmarks.

Day 10: Explore Geneva

Visit the United Nations headquarters, the Jet d'Eau, and enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 11: Departure

Check out from your hotel and depart for the airport. End of the tour.
