About Bern

Bern, the capital city of Switzerland, is known for its well-preserved medieval architecture. The Old Town of Bern, a UNESCO World Heritage site, features narrow streets, arcades, and fountains dating back to the 15th century. The Federal Palace (Bundeshaus), home to the Swiss Federal Assembly and Federal Council, stands prominently in the heart of the city.

Visitors to Bern can enjoy panoramic views of the city from the Rosengarten, a beautiful rose garden perched on a hillside.
