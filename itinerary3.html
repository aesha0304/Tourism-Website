Lakeside Escape Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Geneva

Arrive in Geneva and check in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day exploring the city's landmarks and getting acclimated.

Day 2: Explore Geneva

Visit the United Nations Headquarters, the Jet d'Eau, and stroll through the city's beautiful parks and museums.

Day 3: Travel to Montreux

Travel to Montreux by train. Explore the vineyards and castles around Lake Geneva, and visit the famous Chillon Castle.

Day 4: Boat Trip on Lake Geneva

Enjoy a scenic boat trip on Lake Geneva, taking in the breathtaking views and relaxing on the water.

Day 5: Explore Lausanne and Departure

Travel to Lausanne and explore the cultural heritage of the city. Stroll along the lakeside promenade before departing for the airport.
